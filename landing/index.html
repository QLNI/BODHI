Raise a brain. Send it to fight. Win the company.

03 · The five pillars

What a real brain actually does.

◉

Perception

Walsh–Hadamard Transform. Integer math. 96% compression. Patented image + audio codecs.

♥

Emotion

Every memory carries a valence from −255 to +255. Emotion gates what gets remembered.

✺

Memory

10,000 concept fingerprints + 632 audio fingerprints, all retrievable by pattern-match.

★

Drive

Seven homeostatic drives — hunger, curiosity, safety, social, rest, play, purpose.

☽

Sleep

Five phases. Hebbian replay. LoRA rank-8 nightly fine-tune. No dream, no learning.

▸

Voice

40M-param int8 Broca transformer converts the brain's activation trace into English.
